Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At BYOND, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This policy explains our practices in detail.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BYOND, a wellness and productivity application designed to help you achieve your goals through journaling, task management, community support, and personal challenges. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), profile name, and profile picture
  • Journal Entries: Your personal journal content, mood selections, and timestamps
  • Task Data: Goals, tasks, subtasks, checklists, completion status, and calendar events
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, discussions, and interactions within community channels
  • Challenge Participation: Challenge selections, progress tracking, and completion data
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system (we do not store your payment details)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location)

2.3 Third-Party Authentication

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your public profile information (name and profile picture)
  • A unique identifier to authenticate your account

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve BYOND's features and functionality
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Communication: To send you notifications, reminders, updates, and community activity alerts
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Subscription Management: To manage your premium subscription and billing
  • Community Features: To enable social interactions, discussions, and community engagement
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Firebase and Cloud Storage

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, which include:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure user management
  • Cloud Firestore for app data storage
  • Firebase Cloud Storage for media files
  • Industry-standard encryption both in transit and at rest

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure password hashing and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Automated backup systems

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., Google Firebase, Apple services)
  • Community Features: Your profile information and community posts are visible to other BYOND users within community channels
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or governmental request
  • Safety and Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of BYOND, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice provided)

5.3 Private by Default

Your journal entries, personal tasks, and mood data are private by default and are never shared with other users or third parties unless you explicitly choose to share them.

6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal data we have collected about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download a copy of your data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in your device settings

6.2 Managing Your Data

You can manage your data directly within the app:

  • Edit or delete journal entries
  • Modify or remove tasks and goals
  • Update profile information
  • Leave community channels
  • Delete community posts and comments

7. Push Notifications

We use push notifications to send you:

  • Task and goal reminders
  • Challenge updates and milestones
  • Community activity notifications
  • App updates and announcements

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app preferences.

8. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy governs the processing of payment information.

Subscription Details:

  • Premium Monthly: $9.99/month
  • Premium Yearly: $99.99/year

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.

9. Children's Privacy

BYOND is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

BYOND integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and cloud storage (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Services: For In-App Purchases and notifications (Apple Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain information for legal or regulatory purposes.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of BYOND after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

15. Contact Us

Questions About This Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@byondapp.xyz

We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
